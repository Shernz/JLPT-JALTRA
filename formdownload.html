JLPT Instructions for filling up the form

    Dear Applicant,
    Greetings of the Day
  1. Applicants can directly type their information onto this Fillable PDF version of the Application Form and Test Voucher (hereinafter referred to as “Fillable PDF Forms”) instead of writing by hand on a printed Application Form. Therefore, fewer writing errors are expected to occur.
  2. Please download the JLPT JULY 2020 writable PDF Application form from our website www.jlptchennaiind.com / www.abkaotschennai.com / www.jlptchennaiindia.com from 10.00 am of Monday 2nd March 2020 till 5.00 pm of Friday 3rd April 2020. The cost of Test fee is indicated in the Table under clause 8. Also download the Test Guide and Instruction sheet. Kindly go through in detail from page 21 to 30 of Test Guide and the instruction sheet in filling and sending the application form.
  3. If you open the PDF file and Fill up form A by typing in the personal computer (PC) your data will automatically get written in Forms B, C and the test vouchers. Then print all the 5 pages (single sheet) in a A4 size sheet to be sent to us as instructed down below. If you fill up by writing, you will have to write in all the five pages.
  4. It is mandatory that the applicant SHOULD SIGN the application by himself or herself. The exceptions indicated by Japan Foundation are given below. Applicant’s signature is mandatory.
  5. Please fill the application with correct date of birth & name as in the copy of self-attested ID you are asked to send along with the application.
  6. Please paste 2 recent Colour Photos Full face Portrait 3 to 4cms X 3cms with plain back ground on the Test Voucher(受験票 じゅけんひょう)at the appropriate place indicated.
  7. Please send the filled application along with a self-attested ID copy, showing your date of birth (Not year of Birth alone) to be shown to the proctor while entering your test room for verification.
  8. The application with the ID copy must be accompanied by a Demand Draft (D.D) as indicated below in the name of ABK - AOTS DOSOKAI, TAMILNADU CENTER payable at Chennai if sent by Courier / Postal service or can be paid in cash if submitted in person to ABK AOTS DOSOKAI, Tamilnadu Centre, 3rd Floor, Chateau D Ampa, New No. 37 Old No.110, Nelson Manickam Road, Aminjikarai, Chennai, Tamil Nadu 600029. Please do not send by e mail.

  9. In case you tick in the Application Form the Box for “Request Special Testing Accommodations” then please send also a request letter for special arrangement along with the application.
  10. Kindly note that the Maximum Number of filled in applications we can receive, all 5 levels put together is ONLY 3500. As we review, scrutinize and accept the applications, our site will also have a window showing the current running number accepted against our handling capacity of 3500. There will be No separate Level wise allocation.
  11. We will verify and accept only completed forms without any Defect or missing entry. Please check with the Test Guide (Page 21 to30).
  12. Though there is a dead line for submitting the forms, we will also be limiting the receipt of correctly filled in application forms “ON THE FIRST COME FIRST SERVED BASIS”, as received at our office, only up to 3500, which may be even before the dead line.
  13. If any defective entry or missing entry or a bad photograph or no Copy of ID, the form will be returned to the applicant. When the applicant resubmits the form, the same will be accepted after verification, only as indicated in Clause 11. On the day of re-submission after rectifying the defects, the application will be accepted only if there is a slot on or before 3rd April 2020.
  14. Once the application, the ID and Photographs with good clarity, is found correct, like the earlier years the Form C and Test Voucher will be sent by courier / postal service and a confirmation mail will be sent to the email ID provided by the applicant for information.
